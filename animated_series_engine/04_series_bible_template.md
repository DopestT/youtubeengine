## WORKED EXAMPLE — *Sprout*

### Title
**Sprout** · tagline: *"Soon… so very soon."*

### Logline
A tiny, overconfident houseplant who's certain it's destined to rule the world wants
global domination, but it literally cannot move, so every episode it schemes to
manipulate the humans around it — and it goes wrong.

### World
- **One weird law:** Sprout is fully sentient and speaks (to the audience); no human can
  hear it, and no human ever notices anything is off.
- **What's normal here:** an ordinary apartment, ordinary people, one extraordinary plant.
- **Consequences:** if Sprout is moved, watered wrong, or upstaged by the cat, the plan
  collapses.
- **Status system:** "phases of the master plan" (Sprout narrates which phase it's on).
- **Reset rule:** every episode ends with Sprout back in its pot, undefeated in its own
  mind, already planning the next phase.

### Main Characters
1. **Sprout** — Schemer · trait: grandiose · want: world domination · flaw: it's a plant
   and can't move · silhouette: small leafy sprout with a tiny intense face · color:
   signature acid-green · voice tic: theatrical villain monologue, over-enunciated.
2. **The Owner ("Dave")** — Oblivious Straight Man · trait: cheerfully clueless · exists
   to unknowingly foil or accidentally help the plan.
3. **The Cat ("Mittens")** — Rival · the only one who *sees through* Sprout; silent menace,
   ownable orange.

### Side Characters
- **The Neighbor** — occasional Wildcard who disrupts plans by random visits.
- **The Roomba** — recurring "minion" Sprout tries (and fails) to recruit.

### Villain / Conflict
- **Primary engine:** Schemer's plan vs. the obvious flaw (it can't move).
- **Recurring antagonist:** the Cat — same domestic territory, simply better at everything,
  never says a word.

### Episode Format
- **Length:** 35–60s.
- **Beat sheet:** villain monologue hook → reveal today's plan → 2 escalations →
  the flaw/cat ruins it → deadpan button → tag: *"Soon… so very soon."*
- **Format episodes:** (a) "Sprout's Plan #N", (b) "Sprout tries to recruit a minion",
  (c) "Sprout reacts to [household event] as if it's a world-historical threat".

### Visual Style
- Flat 2D, thick-line, limited palette. Signature acid-green. One static set.
- **Brand stamp:** end card zooms on Sprout's tiny face + green title card with the
  catchphrase.

### Voice Style
- Deadpan, theatrical villain VO for Sprout (AI VO: low pitch, slow, grandiose). Humans
  mostly mumble/ambient. Cat is silent.

### Running Jokes
- Sprout assigns absurd "phases" to mundane events.
- Sprout keeps trying to recruit the Roomba.
- The Cat foils everything by doing nothing.
- **Catchphrase:** *"Soon… so very soon."*

### Emotional Core
A tiny thing that desperately wants to matter and never will — and keeps trying anyway.
That's the part people put on a shirt.

### First 10 Episode Ideas
1. Sprout's plan to "control the weather" (it's just the thermostat).
2. Sprout tries to recruit the Roomba as a war machine.
3. Sprout attempts to blackmail Dave (it can't hold the camera).
4. Sprout vs. the Cat: the staring contest for "dominion over the windowsill."
5. Sprout interprets being repotted as a "kidnapping by enemy forces."
6. Sprout's scheme to get more sunlight (= world energy supremacy).
7. Sprout tries to send a threatening letter (it can't reach the pen).
8. Sprout recruits a fly. The fly leaves.
9. Sprout's "phase one" of global broadcast = Dave's TV turns on by accident.
10. Sprout almost wins, then gets watered and forgets the plan.

### First 3 Full Scripts

**SCRIPT 1 — "Phase One"** (≈40s)
- **[0–2s HOOK]** *Tight on Sprout's tiny menacing face.* SPROUT (VO, grand): "Today… I
  begin my conquest of the world." Caption: *Day 1 of world domination.*
- **[2–10s SETUP]** *Pull back: it's a sad little plant on a windowsill.* SPROUT: "Phase
  one: seize control of the central climate apparatus." *Cut to a thermostat across the
  room.*
- **[10–25s ESC 1]** SPROUT: "I need only… reach it." *Sprout strains. Leaves wiggle one
  millimeter.* SPROUT: "...The first inch of many."
- **[25–40s ESC 2 + TURN]** *Dave walks in, bumps the thermostat to 72°.* SPROUT (awed):
  "It obeys. The humans already bend to my will." *Dave waters Sprout, walks off.*
- **[40s BUTTON + TAG]** SPROUT (drenched, undeterred): "Phase two begins tomorrow." *End
  card, green, catchphrase:* **"Soon… so very soon."**

**SCRIPT 2 — "The Minion"** (≈45s)
- **[HOOK]** *Sprout glaring down at a Roomba.* SPROUT: "Every empire needs an army."
  Caption: *Recruitment day.*
- **[SETUP]** SPROUT: "You, machine. Join me. Together we are unstoppable."
- **[ESC 1]** *The Roomba bonks into the wall, reverses.* SPROUT: "...A bold first
  maneuver."
- **[ESC 2]** *Roomba docks itself to charge.* SPROUT: "Resting before the war. Wise."
- **[TURN]** *The Cat hops on the Roomba and rides away.* SPROUT: "...That was MY general."
- **[BUTTON + TAG]** SPROUT: "No matter. I have… a fly." *A fly leaves the frame.* Beat.
  **"Soon… so very soon."**

**SCRIPT 3 — "The Repotting"** (≈50s)
- **[HOOK]** *Hands lift Sprout out of its pot.* SPROUT (panicked-grand): "Unhand me! Do
  you know who I AM?" Caption: *A declaration of war.*
- **[SETUP]** SPROUT: "Kidnapped. By enemy forces. Predictable."
- **[ESC 1]** *Dave gently moves it to a bigger, nicer pot.* SPROUT: "A larger
  throne-room. They fear me."
- **[ESC 2]** *More sunlight hits the new spot.* SPROUT: "And tribute — the sun itself
  bows."
- **[TURN]** *The Cat knocks the old empty pot off the sill; Dave panics about the cat,
  ignores Sprout entirely.* SPROUT: "...My nemesis grows bold."
- **[BUTTON + TAG]** SPROUT (basking, smug): "Let them think they've won." **"Soon… so
  very soon."**

### Channel Description
Meet Sprout — the world's smallest, most overconfident supervillain. He has a master plan
to rule the world. He also can't move, because he's a houseplant. Every episode, Sprout
schemes from his windowsill, narrates his "phases of conquest," and is foiled by a cat, a
Roomba, or a man named Dave who has no idea any of this is happening. New episodes every
[cadence]. Subscribe and watch the takeover unfold — *soon… so very soon.*

### Social Bios
- **YouTube:** "World domination. One windowsill at a time. 🌱 New eps [cadence]."
- **TikTok:** "tiny plant. big plans. 🌱 soon… so very soon."
- **Instagram:** "Supervillain (houseplant). Cannot move. Will rule the world. 🌱"
- **X:** "Plotting global domination from a pot. Updates between schemes. 🌱"

### Thumbnail Style
Extreme close-up on Sprout's tiny menacing face, acid-green background, 2–3 word
big-caps text (e.g. "PHASE ONE"), high contrast, same font every time.

### Title Style
Formula: **"Sprout's Plan to ___"** or **"Sprout tries to ___"**.

### Posting Schedule
Start 1×/day on TikTok (hook testing) + YouTube Shorts; 4×/week on Reels; repurpose best
clip to X. Evening posts. Reassess cadence against the scorecard after 2 weeks.
